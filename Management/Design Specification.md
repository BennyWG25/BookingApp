The software client, Mad Swans, may be referred to as the "admin" for clarity.
The customers of Mad Swans will be referred to as the "guests". Their experience with the app is also essential and is broken down in its own detail.

## Admin needs
- Rules based pricing and availability (MVP)
- Analytics of bookings
- Guest profiles, with membership levels 
- GDPR compliance (MVP)
- Colour coded bookings 
- Tracking of lended clubs, check in/out, assign to bookings
- Discount codes/ support glu gift vouchers
- Lightspeed integration (MVP)
- Mews integration (MVP)
- Ability to add services/availability easily
- Able to block out bays as "busy" (MVP)

## Guests' Needs
- Easy and secure booking portal (MVP)
- Able to select bays/specify adjacent
- Padel and golf events/tournaments 
- Membership management 
- All sports are easy to book
